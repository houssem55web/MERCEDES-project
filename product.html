Mercedes-Benz EQE Berline

Type: BEV

From USD 97,760 $

The Mercedes-Benz EQE Berline is a fully electric luxury sedan, part of the EQ family. It offers a balance of comfort, advanced tech, and sustainable driving.

Powered by a BEV platform, the EQE offers rear-wheel and all-wheel drive options. Range is up to 660 km (WLTP) with fast charging capability. AMG models deliver high performance with sporty tuning.

Features include the advanced MBUX Hyperscreen, premium materials, spacious seating, and cutting-edge driver assistance systems for a modern luxury experience.
